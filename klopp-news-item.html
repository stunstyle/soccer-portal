Jurgen Klopp sends warning to Mohamed Salah and Sadio Mane over Golden Boot race

  1. Home
  2. News
  3. Jurgen Klopp sends warning to Mohamed Salah and Sadio Mane over Golden Boot race

The two Liverpool frontmen both netted twice in Friday’s win over Huddersfield.

Posted on April 10, 2019 at 10:00 AM by Ivan Stoev

Jurgen Klopp says he will speak to Mohamed Salah and Sadio Mane about toning down their efforts to win the Premier League Golden Boot as both players bagged braces on Friday night.

The Egyptian took his tally for the season to 21 goals in the 5-0 demolition of Huddersfield, while Mane is just one behind after netting with two brilliant headers – and hitting the woodward with a third.

Both players looked like they wanted even more goals as the match became more and more open late on, but Klopp wants them to rein in their attacking instincts in future.

Salah scored with a brilliant chip in the first half and added a second late on.

‘It’s difficult. I spoke to them, I didn’t want them to chase back anymore or stuff like that,’ the Liverpool boss explained on Sky Sports when asked about their battle for the Golden Boot.

‘But for sure, in this moment, we are human beings – one has 21 now and the other 20 – so [you can’t] take one off in that moment. Both looked really good.

‘When you see then how Mo is running with each ball you think, “what the heck are you doing?” It’s good, but we need to talk about that obviously, that’s clear.

‘But today was a day where you have to leave them on the pitch and manage the game in a very offensive way, which they did obviously.’
