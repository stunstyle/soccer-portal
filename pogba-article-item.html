European Paper Talk: Paul Pogba already in talks with Real Madrid

  1. Home
  2. Articles
  3. European Paper Talk: Paul Pogba already in talks with Real Madrid

Posted on March 9, 2019 at 18:29 PM by Georgi Martinov

Marca report Real Madrid are already in talks with Paul Pogba

Spain

Paul Pogba plans to leave Manchester United this summer and has already started negotiations with Real Madrid. The World Cup winner, who was named in the PFA Team of the Year, has reportedly refused to fill in his VISA application for United's pre-season tour of the States as he looks to push through a move. (Marca)

After the appointment of Zinedine Zidane, Real Madrid players are now determined to stay at the Bernabeu. It was reported that World Cup-winning defender Raphael Varane intended to look for a new challenge in the summer but the 26-year-old is now content with the direction of the club. (Marca)

Atletico Madrid are now tired of Diego Costa's antics and are prepared to sell the forward to the highest bidder this summer. After being red-carded for a foul-mouthed rant during what many believed to be the title decider against Barcelona, Diego Simeone now feels as though the 30-year-old striker's time at the club is over. Especially with Alvaro Morata and Antoine Griezmann clicking in the final third for Atleti. (Mundo Deportivo)

Italy

Paul Pogba plans to leave Manchester United this summer and has already started negotiations with Real Madrid. The World Cup winner, who was named in the PFA Team of the Year, has reportedly refused to fill in his VISA application for United's pre-season tour of the States as he looks to push through a move. (Marca)

West Ham and Watford are among a host of clubs monitoring Juan Cuadrado's situation at Juventus. The 30-year-old has seen his playing time in Turin take a hit since the arrival of Cristiano Ronaldo and a move could be on the cards. (Tuttosport)

If AC Milan fail to qualify for the Champions League they may be forced to sell a number of stars in the summer. Alessio Romagnoli could be the big name casualty with the centre-back eager to test himself in Europe's elite competition. The 24-year-old is valued at between £60-70mn. (Calciomercato)
