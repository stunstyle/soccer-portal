CM: Davide Calabria on Manchester United’s transfer wish list

  1. Home
  2. News
  3. CM: Davide Calabria on Manchester United’s transfer wish list

Posted on April 2, 2019 at 18:29 PM

Manchester United will most likely need a new right back in the summer as Antonio Valencia is set to leave the club and Ashley Young is not doing great as of late.

Calciomercato report that among the names United have in mind are the likes of Thomas Meunier and Aaron Wan-Bissaka, but the idea of bringing Calabria in Manchester may be considered as well.

The Premier League side are hoping that Calabria won’t renew his contract with Milan and they can get in the race for his signature, though it’s hard to see the young defender leave the San Siro side.

In fact, the Italian starlet has a contract with the Italian giants till 2022 and current reports suggest that Leonardo wants to further extend it till 2024 with the player likely to agree.
